About

Myself

I was motivated to opt for Computer Science since I had an innate interest in exploring its various concepts. I assimilated the true potential and significance of Computer Science only when I volunteered during Chennai floods in 2015 and witnessed the role played by social media platforms such as Facebook and WhatsApp in effectively distributing the resources from the donor to the needy. This episode led me to understand the immense possibilities promised by the realms of Computer Science to make this world a better place.


I am passionate about developing applications that will make people's daily lives much better.


I have a keen interest in Full Stack development to build large scale distributed systems.


Currently I am pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science at Northeastern University.


Through this opportunity I want to enhance my knowledge and skills in the fields that I am interested in and most importantly to improve myself as a better individual in all aspects.


I am an avid food lover and a die-hard MS Dhoni fan.
